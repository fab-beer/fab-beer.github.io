Support bars & restaurants

By advocating for MA legislation to allow take-away sales during the Covid 19 Crisis!



The plan:

Our favorite local bars and restaurants are suffering from a completely unprecedented challenge to afford their costs and pay staff during the current Covid 19 medical crisis. Understanding the current extreme circumstances, several states including NY and Maine have enacted emergency legislation in order to protect business owners and help them pay employees by letting on-premises establishments sell alcohol at their storefronts and via delivery to customers to consume in their homes.

Together with Somerville's rep Denise Provost and our favorite establishments, FAB asks Governor Charlie Baker to sign an emergency executive order which will allow bars and restaurants to add delivery and take-away alcohol in closed containers to their menu, adding revenue to take-away orders and helping to support staff and pay costs.
