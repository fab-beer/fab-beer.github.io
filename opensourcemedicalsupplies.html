The plan:

To support our local makerspaces and address medical supply shortages, around the globe, FAB is partnering up with opensourcemedicalsupplies.org globally, and the Artisan's Asylum here in Somerville. Profits from the sale of our upcoming "FAB 100" (hundred day anniversary beer) will be contributed to funding the local effort at the Asylum.
