FAB has brewed our first beers!

These first two recipes are called BALLOON FACTORY and SQUID PRO QUO, and each can is inspired by artworks from Adam O'Day and Andrew Jacob respectively. Both beers are mash-hopped, dry-hopped with Citra, and fermented with west coast-style Chico yeast. BALLOON FACTORY is a 6.1% ABV American IPA and SQUID PRO QUO is a Double IPA.

Both beers are on target for release in the week of December 15th.
